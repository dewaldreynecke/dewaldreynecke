What I do

I build custom software solutions for small and medium companies. Why? With modern tooling it is possible for a single developer to build fully functional applications that deliver a lot of value. That brings the cost down to reasonable levels where smaller companies (or sole entrepeneurs!) can afford it.

Using my years of experience analysing and improving internal workflows, combined with with my code writing expertise I will help your small business operate more smoothly and easily.

Why choose me to do it for you

  • Confidentiality

    I strongly believe that your information is yours alone, so will not be sharing anything I may see or learn at your business with anyone. That is also why this website contains no tracking or analytics.

  • Business before code

    Thoroughly understanding the business requirements and necessary outcomes before writing the first line of code leads to better outcomes. Using my experience analysing workflows and my project managment training I will ensure the right things happen. Though I like writing code it is not the primary goal of our engagement.

  • Clean simple code

    Software lives on and will invitably have to be maintained. Whether it turns out to be me or someone else, they will benefit from the simply structured and well documented project I put together, easing the maintance burden and cost.

How I work

To address the unmet expectations and overruns that so often plague software projects we will fix the scope through agreement on expected outcomes at handover, along with a fixed price.

You are protected from cost overruns typical with hourly billing and we are both incentivised to keep the scope and timeline in check.

User training will complete phase 1.

After the initial build we can enter another phase to address the additional requirements that became clear during the build, if any.
